Solar Remote Monitoring System

It has been increasingly getting difficult to monitor the power generated from the solar power generation system in real-time. How about having a system to remotely monitor the performance of the power generation continuously and to ensure the smooth operations of the system? With our Solar Remote Monitoring System, this is made possible.

ivetel, pioneers in the field of making solar power products, provides solution to monitor the power generated from the solar power generation system. Solar Remote monitoring system also comes with a policing and diagnosing feature which provides remedy swiftly.

Our Solar Remote Monitoring System continuously monitors the key performance metrics of the power generation unit and displays the power generated by the system in real-time. The end-users can track and monitor the entire spectrum of the power generation and utilization operations either through web applications or through kiosks.

Incessant monitoring of system parameters such as Solar cumulative production, Solar power generation and load consumption can be made possible now hassle free with our Solar Remote monitoring system dashboard. Statistical data provided by our Solar Remote monitoring system is a boon to the users which facilitates to view the trend of panel power, Solar Energy, voltage and current.

ePowerAudit, another fascinating feature in the Solar Remote Monitoring System monitors the energy consumption and assists the user to utilize the energy in a smarter way.

The innovative ePowerAudit of the Solar Remote Monitoring system tracks the electricity consumption throughout the day which eventually helps you to assimilate the energy usage patterns. This can in turn help you to take informed decisions about the power generation and consumption process.

Value for every part of the Solar Energy Project Chain

SRM solution brings value to each piece of the solar chain. We have developed our solution to address the needs of the system owner, and integrator in one effective package.

End User - The end user benefits by gaining the ability to track their systems performance use our web and kiosk views to enhance their supply usage.

Integrator - The integrator or contractor benefits by being able to keep track of all their valued customers in one place, quickly, and efficiently. Alarms and notices can alert the integrator to potential system problems and outages instantly, and troubleshooting features can save time for both contractor and customer. Public and web displays can be a powerful tool to generate interest from new clients and provide a showcase of past projects.


Manage your Electricity Production and Consumption

SRM has another innovative technology called ePowerAudit that measures how your family uses electricity throughout the day. Understanding your energy usage patterns can help your family reduce your energy costs by informing smarter choices on how and when you use electricity.
